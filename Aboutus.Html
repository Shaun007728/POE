History

The Organisation was founded in the year of 2019 by MR L.S Manda. The business specialises in supplying clean water and bottled water to the community of emondlo and surrounding areas in KwaZuluNatal

Mission

The primary mission of the business is to provide clean and drinkable water at a cheap price . The business aims at building the infrastrucre of the community by allowing people to pour back into the business so the business can grow alongside the community.

Vision

The main vision of the business is to ensure sustainable access to clean ,safe and affordable water for the community , while protecting and preserving our natural resources for future generations

Location

The business is located in the township of Emondlo in Block C84 alongside the main road. Deliveries are done all over KZN.
